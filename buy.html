SECURE TRANSACTIONS and SSL:
 By "secure," we mean that the NowPromos web site uses the SSL protocol. Our web servers are capable of delivering 128-bit encryption. However, your browser, not by the server, ultimately determines the exact level of encryption. For example, if your browser is capable of communicating using 128 bits, the server will use 128-bit encryption. If your browser is only able to communicate using 40-bit encryption, then the server will use 40-bit encryption.Since online commerce sites deal with sensitive data all transmissions containing personal data using the Secured Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol. You may not be able to shop online if your browser does not support SSL or if you are accessing the internet from behind a firewall that prohibits SSL transactions.

When this occurs NowPromos will bill your credit card ONLY if the sample is not returned after your 30-day evaluation period.   If the sample is returned in saleable condition prior to the 30-day evaluation period you will not be charged.  Samples decorated with your logo or spec samples made to order are not returnable under any circumstances.
